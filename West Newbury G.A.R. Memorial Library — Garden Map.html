About the Library Pollinator Garden

490 Main St, West Newbury, Massachusetts 01985
Garden located behind the library
The West Newbury GAR Library Pollinator Garden is a collaboration between the Gulf of Maine Institute (GOMI), the West Newbury GAR Library and West Newbury Wild and Native (WN2). Under the direction of the Library's Director's and working with students from the Pentucket Regional School District, implementation of the native garden was begun by GOMI in 2021 under a NOAA grant. Non-native shrubs were removed and an early garden layout developed. The following year, initial plantings were installed by students. In 2024, the WN2 Native Demonstration Garden Committee donated and planted more natives, installed a walkway and plant markers and continues to frame and maintain the garden.
