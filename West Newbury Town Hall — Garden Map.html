West Newbury Town Hall Pollinator Garden

381 Main St, West Newbury, MA 01985
The West Newbury Town Hall Pollinator Garden is a collaborative project between West Newbury Wild and Native (WN2) and the West Newbury Garden Club and was planted in 2023. The garden was designed to keep the architectural elements at the base of the building visible per the West Newbury Historical Society's request. The geometric shape of this pollinator garden mimics the lines of the building for a more formal design. These low growing native plants chosen for this sunny, dry location, benefit bees, butterflies, moths and other wildlife.
